# 💳 Paisabazaar-Banking-Fraud-Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer financial and behavioral data to understand the key factors affecting credit scores. The goal is to help financial institutions improve risk assessment and decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
To identify patterns in customer financial behavior and determine the key drivers of credit score classification for better loan approval strategies.

---

## 📊 Dataset Description
The dataset includes:
- Income and salary details
- Credit utilization ratio
- Outstanding debt
- Payment delays
- Number of loans and credit cards
- Behavioral features like payment patterns

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- High **credit utilization (>70%)** leads to poor credit scores  
- Frequent **payment delays** significantly increase risk  
- High **debt-to-income ratio** indicates financial stress  
- Customers with **balanced financial behavior** have better scores  

---

## 📈 Exploratory Data Analysis

- Performed **20+ visualizations**
- Used **Univariate, Bivariate, and Multivariate analysis**
- Identified strong relationships between financial behavior and credit score

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Implement **risk-based segmentation**
- Monitor **high credit utilization users**
- Track **payment behavior patterns**
- Limit loans for high EMI customers
- Provide **personalized financial advice**
